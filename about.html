About

Clinamenic LLC is a consultancy and freelance service-provider, specializing in virtual organization design and governance. Using cutting-edge open-source technology, organizations are able to operate and coordinate at new levels of transparency and composability, and Clinamenic LLC seeks to help organizations implement such technology.

Virtual organization design?

From C-suite to community, all organizations have structure and some form of hierarchy, however vertically or horizontally integrated. Given the advent of smart contracts, certain organizational structures, such as shared accounts and shareholder bodies, can now be represented by open-source algorithms which can be transparently and collectively governed.
