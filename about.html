ABOUT

Founded and operated by Spencer Saar Cavanaugh, Clinamenic LLC is a solo consultancy and freelance service provider, specializing in the design, governance, and operations of onchain organizations.

Using cutting-edge open-source technology, organizations are able to operate and coordinate at new levels of transparency and composability, and Clinamenic LLC seeks to help organizations implement such technology.

    CONTEXT

    Virtual organization design?

    From C-suite to community, all organizations have structure and some form of hierarchy, however vertically or horizontally integrated.

    Given the advent of smart contracts, certain organizational structures, such as shared accounts and shareholder bodies, can now be represented by open-source algorithms which can be transparently and collectively governed.

    These algorithms can be considered building blocks for organizations.
