ABOUT

Founded and operated by Spencer Saar Cavanaugh, Clinamenic LLC is a solo consultancy and freelance service provider, specializing in virtual organization design and governance.

Using cutting-edge open-source technology, organizations are able to operate and coordinate at new levels of transparency and composability, and Clinamenic LLC seeks to help organizations implement such technology.
