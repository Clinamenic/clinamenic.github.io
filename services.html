S E R V I C E S

Clinamenic LLC specializes in the design of virtual organizations and the establishment of governance frameworks, and is available for hourly consultations and for service contracts. Please use the calendar below to schedule a call.

Hourly

Hourly consultations are billed upon completion, at a rate of $100 per hour, with a month-long invoice period.

If you are not satisfied with the knowledge exchange of the consultation, no costs will be incurred.

Contract

Clinamenic LLC is available for contractual engagements, in the form of a retainer or in terms of deliverables.

If you are interested in booking a discovery call, to discuss the prospects of such an engagement, the hourly cost will be waived.
