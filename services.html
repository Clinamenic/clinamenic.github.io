    OPERATIONS

    If your organization needs to adopt onchain tooling to establish joint accounts, manage web3 social media profiles, or facilitate other operations, Clinamenic LLC can help implement bespoke solutions, and coach your team on the usage of these tools.

    Build a dynamic hierarchy of roles, each with its own particular permissions, and manage their payroll onchain.

    Delegate access to web3 social media accounts, or enable your community to elect its social media manager.

    Run a fully transparent grants program within an onchain organization, or enable your community to run one.

    Engage with contractors via onchain escrow accounts which have built-in dispute resolution mechanisms.
